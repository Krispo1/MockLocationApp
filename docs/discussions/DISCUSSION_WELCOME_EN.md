# 👋 Welcome to MockLocationApp Discussions!

This space is for users, contributors, and testers who want to:

- Ask questions about how the app works
- Suggest new features
- Share feedback or ideas
- Help others and co-create documentation

---

## 📌 If you're new:
- Introduce yourself
- Describe how you use the app
- Ask a question or share an idea

## 🧑‍💻 If you're a contributor:
- Review `README.md`, `CONTRIBUTING.md`, and `LICENSE`
- Use tags like `question`, `idea`, `bug`, `discussion` when starting threads

---

## 🛡️ Moderation

This community is moderated. We encourage openness, respect, and collaboration.  
Posts that violate the guidelines will be removed.
